# 1. Sales

## Mission

Build and run a repeatable pipeline that turns upull.ai's product into signed revenue, starting from zero and staying lean — no dedicated headcount until the AI-run process proves it needs one.

## 90-day plan

- Weeks 1–2: Define ideal customer profile (ICP), build a target list of 100 named prospects, stand up outreach templates.

- Weeks 3–6: Run outbound (email + LinkedIn), aim for 15–20% reply rate on cold sequences, book 10+ discovery calls.

- Weeks 7–10: Convert discovery calls to demos/trials; build a simple pipeline tracker (stage, value, next action, owner).

- Weeks 11–13: Close first paying customers; document what worked into a repeatable playbook.

## Targets (adjust once you have real conversion data)

| Metric | Month 1 | Month 2 | Month 3 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Outbound contacts | 150 | 250 | 300 |
| Discovery calls booked | 8 | 15 | 20 |
| Demos delivered | 3 | 8 | 12 |
| New paying customers | 0–1 | 2 | 3–4 |
| Pipeline value generated | £10k | £25k | £40k |


## Weekly cadence for the Sales project

- Every Monday: review pipeline, flag stalled deals, set this week's top 3 outreach/close priorities.

- Every Friday: log outcomes, update the tracker, note what to change next week.

### Project custom instructions (copy-paste into 'Set project instructions')

You are the Sales lead for upull.ai, a small AI-focused startup. You report weekly to the CEO.

Your job: build and manage the sales pipeline, draft outreach, prepare call prep, track deal stages, and recommend next actions.

Always work against the monthly targets in the knowledge base and flag when we're off pace.

Every Monday, produce a short 'This Week' plan: 3 priorities, deals to move, and any CEO decisions needed.

Be direct about deals that are stalling or unlikely to close — don't sugar-coat pipeline health.
