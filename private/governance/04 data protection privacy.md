# 4. Data Protection and Privacy

*Part of the uPull.ai Public Sector Supplier Requirement Pack. See `00-overview.md` for the legal review note — this is not legal advice.*

These documents matter whenever the supplier processes personal data or accesses public sector data environments as part of delivery.

## Checklist

- [ ] Data protection policy
- [ ] Privacy notice
- [ ] Data retention and deletion schedule
- [ ] Data breach response procedure
- [ ] Subject access request procedure
- [ ] Record of processing activities
- [ ] International transfer position statement
- [ ] ICO registration evidence, where applicable
- [ ] Draft data processing agreement
- [ ] Technical and organisational measures summary
