# 2. Financial and Commercial Evidence

*Part of the uPull.ai Public Sector Supplier Requirement Pack. See `00-overview.md` for the legal review note — this is not legal advice.*

Public sector guidance now includes scrutiny of suppliers' approach to payment in certain larger procurements, with due diligence expected before award.

## Checklist

- [ ] Latest filed accounts, if available
- [ ] Latest management accounts
- [ ] VAT registration details, if registered
- [ ] Bank details and payment controls summary
- [ ] Insurance schedule covering public liability, professional indemnity, employers' liability (where required), and cyber cover if held
- [ ] Evidence of prompt payment systems and subcontractor payment processes where relevant, especially on larger opportunities
- [ ] Pricing governance note and delegated approval levels
