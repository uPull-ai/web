# uPull.ai Public Sector Supplier Requirement Pack — Overview

> **Not legal advice.** This pack is a founder-ready working document set to support early public sector and NHS sales preparation. It reflects current UK public procurement due diligence expectations, NHS supplier security guidance, and the Cabinet Office Model Services Contract context — but it must be reviewed by a qualified UK solicitor before use in any live procurement or contract.

## Purpose

This pack exists to support uPull.ai's early public sector and NHS sales preparation, ahead of bid, framework, pilot, or contract stages with UK public bodies.

## How to use this pack

Use this document set as a working checklist and drafting pack before entering bid, framework, pilot, or contract stages. Under the Procurement Act 2023 supplier guidance and related due diligence materials, buyers may assess:

- Legal standing
- Financial standing
- Capability
- Exclusion grounds
- Supply chain reliability
- Broader assurance evidence

before award. The documents in this pack are organised so each requirement area can be worked on, owned, and tracked separately.

## Legal review note

This pack is a drafting and readiness aid only. The Model Services Contract guidance it draws on expressly states that its documents are intended for procurement specialists and lawyers, should be tailored to the project, and should be used with specific legal advice where needed. **No document in this pack should be relied on in a live procurement or contract without review by a qualified UK solicitor.**
