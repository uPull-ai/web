The Push vs. Pull Dilemma

Successful automation relies on frontline-led pathway transformation.

The Old Way: The 'Push' Model

Organisations deploy generic tools globally and expect staff to adapt blindly.

"You cannot transform a workflow without the people who run it."

Result: low adoption and poor ROI.

The New Way: The 'Pull' Principle

Target a critical workflow, redesign the pathway, and upskill frontline to pull AI into operations.

Result: immediate adoption & self-sustaining growth.
